Of Shadows and Wolves

Shunned for being a Shadowborn, Dylan must reshape herself to win the approval of her destined mate. However, R.I.S.E. Academy harbors secrets and is far from what it appears to be. While juggling her academic respinsiblities, Dylan discovers more mates who reveal the true value of being fated mates.
